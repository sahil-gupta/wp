        </p>

     <div class="step " data-x="-200" data-y="1610" data-rotate-y="7" data-scale="1" data-autoplay="1" data-transition-duration="1200" >

     <div class="step " data-x="200" data-y="1840" data-rotate-y="-7" data-scale="1" data-autoplay="1" data-transition-duration="1200" >

            “Yup,” I said.
        </p>
     </div>
    
     <div class="step " data-x="-200" data-y="2070" data-rotate-y="7" data-scale="1" data-autoplay="1" data-transition-duration="1200" >

        </p>
     </div>
    
     <div class="step " data-x="200" data-y="2300" data-rotate-y="-7" data-scale="1" data-autoplay="1" data-transition-duration="1200" >

        </p>
     </div>

     </div>

        </p>
     </div>

        </p>

        </p>
     </div>
    
     <div class="step " data-x="-200" data-y="3450" data-rotate-y="7" data-scale="1" data-autoplay="1" data-transition-duration="1200" >

            “My kids… my wife,” you said.
        </p>
     </div>
    
     <div class="step " data-x="200" data-y="3680" data-rotate-y="-7" data-scale="1" data-autoplay="1" data-transition-duration="1200" >

            “What about them?”
        </p>
     </div>
    
     <div class="step " data-x="-200" data-y="3910" data-rotate-y="7" data-scale="1" data-autoplay="1" data-transition-duration="1200" >

            “Will they be all right?”
        </p>
     </div>
    
     <div class="step lotsoftext" data-x="200" data-y="4140" data-rotate-y="-7" data-scale="1" data-autoplay="1.45" data-transition-duration="1200" >

            “That’s what I like to see,” I said. “You just died and your main concern is for your family. That’s good stuff right there.”
        </p>
     </div>

        <p>
            You looked at me with fascination. To you, I didn’t look like God. I just looked like some man. Or possibly a woman. Some vague authority figure, maybe. More of a grammar school teacher than the almighty.
        </p>
     </div>
    
     <div class="step lotsoftext" data-x="200" data-y="4600" data-rotate-y="-7" data-scale="1" data-autoplay="4.25" data-transition-duration="1200" >
        <p>
            “Don’t worry,” I said. “They’ll be fine. Your kids will remember you as perfect in every way. They didn’t have time to grow contempt for you. Your wife will cry on the outside, but will be secretly relieved. To be fair, your marriage was falling apart. If it’s any consolation, she’ll feel very guilty for feeling relieved.”
        </p>
     </div>
    
     <div class="step " data-x="-200" data-y="4830" data-rotate-y="7" data-scale="1" data-autoplay="1" data-transition-duration="1200" >
        <p>
            “Oh,” you said. “So what happens now? Do I go to heaven or hell or something?”
        </p>
     </div>
    
     <div class="step " data-x="200" data-y="5060" data-rotate-y="-7" data-scale="1" data-autoplay="1" data-transition-duration="1200" >
        <p>
            “Neither,” I said. “You’ll be reincarnated.”
        </p>
     </div>
    
     <div class="step " data-x="-200" data-y="5290" data-rotate-y="7" data-scale="1" data-autoplay="1" data-transition-duration="1200" >
        <p>
            “Ah,” you said. “So the Hindus were right,”
        </p>
     </div>
    
     <div class="step " data-x="200" data-y="5520" data-rotate-y="-7" data-scale="1" data-autoplay="1" data-transition-duration="1200" >
        <p>
            “All religions are right in their own way,” I said. “Walk with me.”
        </p>
     </div>
    
     <div class="step " data-x="-200" data-y="5750" data-rotate-y="7" data-scale="1" data-autoplay="1" data-transition-duration="1200" >
        <p>
            You followed along as we strode through the void. “Where are we going?”
        </p>
     </div>
    
     <div class="step " data-x="200" data-y="5980" data-rotate-y="-7" data-scale="1" data-autoplay="1" data-transition-duration="1200" >
        <p>
            “Nowhere in particular,” I said. “It’s just nice to walk while we talk.”
        </p>
     </div>
    
     <div class="step lotsoftext" data-x="-200" data-y="6210" data-rotate-y="7" data-scale="1" data-autoplay="2.25" data-transition-duration="1200" >
        <p>
            “So what’s the point, then?” You asked. “When I get reborn, I’ll just be a blank slate, right? A baby. So all my experiences and everything I did in this life won’t matter.”
        </p>
     </div>
    
     <div class="step lotsoftext" data-x="200" data-y="6440" data-rotate-y="-7" data-scale="1" data-autoplay="1.65" data-transition-duration="1200" >
        <p>
            “Not so!” I said. “You have within you all the knowledge and experiences of all your past lives. You just don’t remember them right now.”
        </p>
     </div>
    
     <div class="step lotsoftext" data-x="-200" data-y="6670" data-rotate-y="7" data-scale="1" data-autoplay="5.35" data-transition-duration="1200" >
        <p>
            I stopped walking and took you by the shoulders. “Your soul is more magnificent, beautiful, and gigantic than you can possibly imagine. A human mind can only contain a tiny fraction of what you are. It’s like sticking your finger in a glass of water to see if it’s hot or cold. You put a tiny part of yourself into the vessel, and when you bring it back out, you’ve gained all the experiences it had.
        </p>
     </div>
    
     <div class="step lotsoftext" data-x="200" data-y="6900" data-rotate-y="-7" data-scale="1" data-autoplay="3.25" data-transition-duration="1200" >
        <p>
            “You’ve been in a human for the last 48 years, so you haven’t stretched out yet and felt the rest of your immense consciousness. If we hung out here for long enough, you’d start remembering everything. But there’s no point to doing that between each life.”
        </p>
     </div>
    
     <div class="step " data-x="-200" data-y="7130" data-rotate-y="7" data-scale="1" data-autoplay="1" data-transition-duration="1200" >
        <p>
            “How many times have I been reincarnated, then?”
        </p>
     </div>
    
     <div class="step lotsoftext" data-x="200" data-y="7360" data-rotate-y="-7" data-scale="1" data-autoplay="1.55" data-transition-duration="1200" >

            “Oh lots. Lots and lots. An in to lots of different lives.” I said. “This time around, you’ll be a Chinese peasant girl in 540 AD.”
        </p>
     </div>
    
     <div class="step " data-x="-200" data-y="7590" data-rotate-y="7" data-scale="1" data-autoplay="1" data-transition-duration="1200" >

        </p>
     </div>

        </p>
     </div>
    
     <div class="step " data-x="-200" data-y="8050" data-rotate-y="7" data-scale="1" data-autoplay="1" data-transition-duration="1200" >

        </p>
     </div>

        </p>
     </div>
    
     <div class="step lotsoftext" data-x="-200" data-y="8510" data-rotate-y="7" data-scale="1" data-autoplay="1.85" data-transition-duration="1200" >

        </p>
     </div>
    
     <div class="step lotsoftext" data-x="200" data-y="8740" data-rotate-y="-7" data-scale="1" data-autoplay="1.55" data-transition-duration="1200" >

            “Sure. Happens all the time. And with both lives only aware of their own lifespan you don’t even know it’s happening.”
        </p>
     </div>
    
     <div class="step " data-x="-200" data-y="8970" data-rotate-y="7" data-scale="1" data-autoplay="1" data-transition-duration="1200" >

            “So what’s the point of it all?”
        </p>
     </div>
    
     <div class="step lotsoftext" data-x="200" data-y="9200" data-rotate-y="-7" data-scale="1" data-autoplay="1.65" data-transition-duration="1200" >

        </p>
     </div>

        </p>
     </div>

            “You mean mankind? You want us to mature?”
        </p>
     </div>
    
     <div class="step lotsoftext" data-x="200" data-y="10120" data-rotate-y="-7" data-scale="1" data-autoplay="1.65" data-transition-duration="1200" >

            “No, just you. I made this whole universe for you. With each new life you grow and mature and become a larger and greater intellect.”
        </p>
     </div>
    
     <div class="step " data-x="-200" data-y="10350" data-rotate-y="7" data-scale="1" data-autoplay="1" data-transition-duration="1200" >

        </p>
     </div>
    
     <div class="step " data-x="200" data-y="10580" data-rotate-y="-7" data-scale="1" data-autoplay="1" data-transition-duration="1200" >

        </p>
     </div>
    
     <div class="step " data-x="-200" data-y="10810" data-rotate-y="7" data-scale="1" data-autoplay="1" data-transition-duration="1200" >

        </p>
     </div>
    
     <div class="step " data-x="200" data-y="11040" data-rotate-y="-7" data-scale="1" data-autoplay="1" data-transition-duration="1200" >

        </p>
     </div>

        </p>

        </p>
     </div>

        </p>
